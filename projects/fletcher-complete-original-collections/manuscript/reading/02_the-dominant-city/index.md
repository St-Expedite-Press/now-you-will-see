Fletcher's second 1913 collection, written in London between 1911 and 1912. Where *The Book of Nature* is Mediterranean and pastoral, *The Dominant City* is urban and nocturnal: its subject is London as a force — its crowds, factories, pleasure districts, and restless night. The collection anticipates Imagist urban poetry and the influence of Belgian Symbolists such as Verhaeren. Three sonnets printed solid in the source (DC 018, DC 022, DC 031) have been divided eight and six in the reading edition, each with an intervention note.
