## Original Lineation

O Death, come soon, soon, soon:
Hasten to end my pangs
With the poison of thy fangs:
And let it be as a boon!

Thy body I fasten to mine,
Thy lips I set to my lips:
Desiring the tragic sign
Of an ultimate eclipse.

O corruption, hasten fast!
Come, worms that will gnaw me whole!
My body has broken at last
'Neath my black and bitter soul.

## Context Notes

Three quatrains, ABBA / CDCD / EFEF. l. 19 ('Desiring the tragic sign / Of an ultimate eclipse'): 'eclipse' — the total occultation of the self's light — is an astronomical figure for death.
