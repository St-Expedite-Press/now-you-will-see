## Bug found and patched during execution

**`texgraph pdf text` — Windows encoding crash**

`subprocess.run` with `text=True` defaulted to CP1252 on Windows, causing
`UnicodeDecodeError` on the IA PDF's OCR text. Fixed in
`machinery/src/texgraph/cli.py:pdf_text`: replaced `text=True` with binary
stdout capture and explicit `decode("utf-8", errors="replace")`.

This fix applies to the `pdf text` command. The `pdf info` and `pdf render`
commands were not affected.
