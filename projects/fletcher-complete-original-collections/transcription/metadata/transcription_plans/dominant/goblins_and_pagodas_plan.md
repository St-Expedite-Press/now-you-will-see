## Method

- Render pages with `.\.venv\Scripts\texgraph.exe pdf render`.
- Use OCR for navigation and checking.
- Symphony files are long — render in batches of 5 scan pages.
- Use `series: "The Ghosts of an Old House"` for Section I poems;
  `series: "Symphonies"` for Section II poems.
- `book_part` follows the Part/Section headers as listed above.
- For short poems sharing a page, record the same scan page in both files.
- "Solitude in the City" internal headings: `## I. Words at Midnight`,
  `## II. The Evening Rain`, `## III. Street of Sorrows`, `## IV. Song in the Darkness`.
- Set `status: "transcribed"` only after visual image checking.
