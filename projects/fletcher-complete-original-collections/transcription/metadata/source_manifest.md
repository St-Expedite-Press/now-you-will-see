## Source Intake Rule

Do not transcribe a book until its full source PDF is present and recorded here.
If a PDF is renamed for stability, copy it to the required filename and keep the
source bytes unchanged.

## Raw Directory Layout

- `raw/early_1913/`: five 1913 books used by _The Early Works of John Gould Fletcher_.
- `raw/middle_1915_1928/`: acquired public-domain middle-period source PDFs.
- `raw/late_rights_pending/`: late books requiring source acquisition; permission cleared for all three.
