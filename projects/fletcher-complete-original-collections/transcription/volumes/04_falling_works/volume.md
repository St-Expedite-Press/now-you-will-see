## Volume Argument

The falling volume is not decline as failure. It is descent as gravity:
Fletcher's late movement into place, Arkansas, memory, elegy, and the pressure
of the South. The triple war of the embattled years is over. The resolution has
come. What remains is to inhabit what was always waiting.

_XXIV Elegies_, _South Star_, and _The Burning Mountain_ make the late case.
They also make the acquisition problem: source PDFs for all three are still to
be obtained. Permission for all three is cleared.
