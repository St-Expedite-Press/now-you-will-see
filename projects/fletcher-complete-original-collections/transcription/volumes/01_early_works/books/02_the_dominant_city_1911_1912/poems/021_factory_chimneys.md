# Factory Chimneys

Motionless blood-hued styluses that scrawl on
  the infinite
Shifting surface of sky for ever and ever unrolled,
Man's greatest achievement and failure, through
  infuriate day and through night,
In hieroglyphs rolling and tumbling, red, black,
  purple and gold.
