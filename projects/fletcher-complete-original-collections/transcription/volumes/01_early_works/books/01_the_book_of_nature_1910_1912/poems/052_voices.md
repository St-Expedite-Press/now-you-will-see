# Voices

The voices of the grass are harsh and shrill:
Cold and flute-like are the voices on the hill.
A bodiless sighing is the voice amid the boughs:
Clamorous are the voices where the torrent flows.
A tapping and a clapping are the voices of the
  creek:
And an empty silence is the voice upon the peak.
