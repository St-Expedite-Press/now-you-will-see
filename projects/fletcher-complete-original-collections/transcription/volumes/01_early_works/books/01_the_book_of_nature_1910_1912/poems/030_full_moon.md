# Full Moon

Full moon, and night's great hush
On grass and flower and bush:
The motionless calm of the trees
Standing in shadowy peace
Down the long road, far away
To the white borders of day.

Through a sparkling ocean of air
The moonlight is scattered, where
There is only smiling sleep,
And the stars that move on the deep.
But the flowers then, men say,
Meditate on the day.
