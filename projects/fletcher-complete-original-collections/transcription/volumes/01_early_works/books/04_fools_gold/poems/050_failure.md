# Failure

Some fail because they never chose to strive;
These, the impassive, smiling, sunlit gods:
Some fail because they strove too much to do;
These are the heroes, seed of Hercules:
Some fail because they stood self-filled apart;
These are the bards of deathless prophecy:

But I have failed,--for I reject success,--
Because success is the only failure I know:
Far better death or madness.
