# Towards the Impossible

I have climbed alone
    Till the gulf below is black:
There is no turning back,
I must needs go on.

Nor do I care if I
Ever the top attain:
Climbing gives no pain,
At the top I shall die.

If I stare upon the snow
Till I am blinded--Well,
It is better in Hell
To go as blind men go.
