---
title: "No Serrana"
type: poem
order: 2
---

I have heard men
compare women to spring.
Loose habit.
Dead speech.

I asked
_donde estaba?_
_Where is the cowherd girl?_

She smiled
like someone hearing
the wrong man
knock on the right door.

She said
"that girl wants no part of love."
Meaning herself.
_esa moza no quiere_
_saber nada de amores._

She meant, in fact said:
go back to your saddle.
