## Notes

Publication order among the five 1913 books is editorially optimal, not
day-by-day certain. All five were issued in 1913; a fully certain month-by-month
sequence has not been established.

Permission is cleared for all three falling-volume books. Source acquisition
should begin with University of Arkansas Special Collections, Yale Beinecke,
and Arkansas Studies repositories.
