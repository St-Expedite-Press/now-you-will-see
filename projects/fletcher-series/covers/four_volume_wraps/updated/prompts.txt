Prompts used for updated covers:
Volume I (Early Works): Warm cream background with a dark tree trunk on the left and spidery branches extending across the left half. Two small birds perched on branches. Negative space on the right for title. Use black and cream with light sepia accents. Keep the scene simple, no leaves or extra detail, to match early modernist poster style. 
Volume II (Dominant Works): High-modernist seascape in blue and cream. Tall slender trees on the left, a golden sun radiating across the horizon with rays, and a calm blue sea with a small sailboat and a distant island. Keep right half open for title. Use limited palette of cream, navy blue, gold, and one accent. 
Volume III (Embattled Works): Dynamic scene with a jagged rocky coastline under a cream sky and dark ocean. A rugged tree with bare branches on the left, jagged rocks at the center being struck by waves, and a vintage biplane with smoke trail flying on the right. Use cream, black, and muted blues. Leave room on the right half for title. 
Volume IV (Falling Works): Quiet Arkansas landscape with a dark river winding through hills toward distant mountains. A bare tree on the left with a bird perched. A small golden star in the sky and a lone bird flying on the right. Use cream, dark greens, browns, and muted gold. Keep composition simple with large negative space.
