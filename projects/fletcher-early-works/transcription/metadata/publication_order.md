# Publication Order

The books are arranged in the best-supported editorial order:

1. _The Book of Nature, 1910-1912_
2. _The Dominant City (1911-1912)_
3. _Fire and Wine_
4. _Fool's Gold_
5. _Visions of the Evening_

All five were issued in 1913. A precise day-by-day publication order has not
been guaranteed from the available source evidence, so this order follows the
standard bibliographic/editorial ordering used for the early Fletcher volumes.
