## Editorial Summary

- _The Book of Nature, 1910-1912_: source front matter includes the unnumbered
  dedicatory poem "To M. F. and A. F. T." on scan 9 and contents pages. Back
  matter includes the T. and A. Constable printer colophon on scan 122.
- _The Dominant City (1911-1912)_: source front matter includes the unnumbered
  dedicatory poem "To the French Poets of To-Day" on scan 9 and contents pages;
  back matter includes the Garden City Press colophon on scan 82.
- _Fire and Wine_: source dedication/reader invocation and contents in front
  matter; the Riverside Press printer statement on scan 8 and "To Anyone
  Reading This Book" on scan 9 are transcribed under the book `front_matter/`.
  No back matter signal found.
- _Fool's Gold_: image-only source required visual review. Front matter includes
  title page, an "In Memoriam" page on scan 3, table of contents, and part-title
  pages. Back matter includes a Garden City Press colophon on scan 90. No prose
  preface found in the reviewed front range.
- _Visions of the Evening_: source contents in front matter. The listed
  "Invocation" items are contents-listed poems, not source front matter. Back
  matter includes the Arden Press colophon on scan 52.
- _Irradiations: Sand and Spray_: source preface and contents in front matter.
- _Goblins and Pagodas_: source preface and contents/permissions material in
  front matter.
- _Japanese Prints_: source preface, table of contents, list of illustrations,
  frontispiece, and endleaf material in front matter; illustration handling
  remains an editorial decision.
- _The Tree of Life_: source contents in front matter.
- _Breakers and Granite_: source contents in front matter. "Invocation" is
  contents-listed and should be treated as a poem.
- _Parables_: source frontispiece and contents in front matter.
- _The Black Rock_: source acknowledgment and contents in front matter.
- _Branches of Adam_, _XXIV Elegies_, _South Star_, and _The Burning Mountain_:
  source scan pending; no source matter inventory possible yet.
