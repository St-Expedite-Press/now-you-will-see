# The Hosts of Song

Through day and night and on through many days
  Ever I seek sensations rude and strong.
Like leaves or winds or waves or clouds or rays
  My hosts of song stream endlessly along.

Beyond the keenest joys, beyond the realms of air,
Beyond infinite peace where ended is all care,
Beyond the dark abyss of the most vile despair,
  My hosts of song stream endlessly along.

Sometimes they are a deep and dark-blue pool,
Sometimes a torrent harsh and terrible,
Sometimes a swamp of ennui cold and dull;
But each with all my heart and soul is full.
The infinite only is my bound and rule:
  Stream endlessly along, you hosts of song!
