# Poetic Art

Ask not a purpose of all things,
  No dogma with fixed mind debate,
But loose each mood to beat its wings
  Freely against the bars of fate.

Welcome each sorrow with full heart,
  As freely as you welcome bliss;
Never to flinch is the best art,
  And to receive all, giving is.
