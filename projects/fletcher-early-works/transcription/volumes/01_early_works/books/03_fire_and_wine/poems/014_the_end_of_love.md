# The End of Love

O, I am weary of Love,
  Of Love with the broken wings,
Of Love with the eyelids bound:
  Whose temple is shut and still,
  Alone on a hidden hill,
Far above any sound.

There is a little pool,
And the trailing leaf-sprays cool
  Dip into it, all day.
It whispers to me of rest,
And a slumbering Naiad's breast:
  There will I sink, and stay.
