# Valley and Peak

In the valleys there is balm:
  And yet the valleys seem
To me devoid of calm.
  I am burdened with tragic dream.

But when twilight quietly knocks
  At the door of my soul, then O
To fly to the icy rocks,
  And the eternal snow!
