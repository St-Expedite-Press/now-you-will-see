# Voices of Autumn

The rain spattered the pond,
  The wind shook at the trees:
But late, in the hours beyond,
  The sun came, bringing peace.

Peace to the sodden walks,
  Peace to the shivering trees,
Peace to the withered stalks;
  Everywhere there was peace.
