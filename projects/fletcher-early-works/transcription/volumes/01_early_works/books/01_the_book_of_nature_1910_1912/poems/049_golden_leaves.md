# Golden Leaves

The dead leaves drift on the walks;
  The dead leaves litter the pond;
They float down like mad butterflies,
  Golden hordes to the southward bound.

On the trees in gold clusters they hang:
  On the turf in gold carpets they rest.
The gold of the year swept away
  Into pawnbroker Time's old chest.
