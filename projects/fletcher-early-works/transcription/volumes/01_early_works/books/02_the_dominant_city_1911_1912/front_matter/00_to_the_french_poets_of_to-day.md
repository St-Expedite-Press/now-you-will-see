# To the French Poets of To-Day

Death-agonies, dances, resurrections hurled,
Have made for me, and made for you, a world:
With the red lava-torrents of the suns
Drunken, we dance yet to the rain's dark drums,
And watch the universe, life, birth, and death,
Hanging each instant on a single breath.
