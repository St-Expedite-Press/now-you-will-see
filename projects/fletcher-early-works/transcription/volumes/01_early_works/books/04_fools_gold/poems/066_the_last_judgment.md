# The Last Judgment

Methought I saw the final Judgment Day:
God woke from slumber, forth He swiftly thrust
His lightnings: earth and sea were burned away.
All souls arose, like a great cloud of dust.

Then from His all-enduring throne He spake:
"The honest bodies of beasts, I gladly bless.
I give to them, from ape to crawling snake,
Immortal lengths of bestial happiness.

"Man, man alone, half-body and half-soul,
Half-wise, half-fool, half-dawning and half-night,
Him I condemn, both separately and whole,
The bottomless abyss remove him from My sight!"
