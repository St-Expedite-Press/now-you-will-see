# The Rocket Falling

I fall, I fall, I am all spent and foredone:
Downwards I swoop, dark from the top of my
        course:
I am ended ere I attain content or remorse:
Like life, I am finished ere I was planned or begun.

The dark earth receives me: but yet, ere around
        me flows,
After my sky-piercing song, the cold, silent sorrow
        of night,
I know yet one thought--one star-thought:--It is
        my delight
That triumphant, in a gold rain of glory, I rose.
