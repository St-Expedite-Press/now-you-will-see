# Ambition

Swift to-morrow with my sword
Will I magic kingdoms win:
Forests penetrate within,
Conquer castles at a word.

All the splendour of the sea
Shall the waves cast in my lap:
Songs from islands of good hap
Shall the breezes blow to me.

Great adventures will I prove,
Till, gem-purpled, she of dreams,
Sailing down far tropic streams,
Claims me as the slave of love.

Then I'll fall, who once was strong:
But I'll stand and smite the strings,
And on universal wings
Pass, amid a storm of song.
