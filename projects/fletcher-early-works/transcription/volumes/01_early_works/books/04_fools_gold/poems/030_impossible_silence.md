# Impossible Silence

Others may seek the tumults of the day,
I am in love with silence.  Long ago
I, too, dreamed dreams of colour, movement,
        sound,
Of rhythm broken yet constant as the sea.

But now the beautiful still grey haze has grown:
The canvas where I draw myself the shapes
That please me, but displease all other men:
While solitude and silence seem the best
Of all earth's gifts to my wayward, sullen mood.

Alas, that they are rarest!  Why we wish
Whatever is not, always puzzles me:
Perchance if we could gain it, we might rest:
But in our imperfection, rest is vain:--
Perfection but the idle hope that makes
Endurable, though void, the weight of days.
