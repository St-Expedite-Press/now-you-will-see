# What the Wind Sang

The broad trees shaken together in a great wind
Roar and murmur, laughing: "It is life, it is life":
The nodding flowers and grasses up the hillside
Sing together, steadily: "Birth and love":
But by the pond the little slender sedges
Low and thrilling whisper: "It is death."
