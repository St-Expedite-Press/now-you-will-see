# In the Hour of Death

I have been dead before.  What knew I then
Ere I was born into the world of men?
Since all things back to whence they came return,
What fear of Death, then, do I now discern?
Though parted from him long, we still must be
As friends that once again each other see!
Oldest of all he is: My hand I rest
In his, and go: the oldest friend is best.
