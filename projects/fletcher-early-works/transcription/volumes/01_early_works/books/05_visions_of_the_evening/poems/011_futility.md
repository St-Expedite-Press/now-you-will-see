# Futility

When I waken in the morning,
    Life seems an adventure gay:
Ere the night, with eyes of emerald,
Comes, I weary of the day.

In the morning I stand ready:
    Golden shines my perfect goal.
Soon the sooty claws of sorrow,
    Seize my naked shivering soul:

I am dragged, spent and exhausted,
    Towards the rubbish-heap of grey,
Where I wait for night to hide me,
    For I weary of the day.
