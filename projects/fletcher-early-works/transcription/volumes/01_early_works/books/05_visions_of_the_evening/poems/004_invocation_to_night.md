# Invocation to Night

Be sensual, be mysterious, O my Night:
Let not the day end in a blaze of light,
But overwhelm it in your ashy flood
Slowly, as dying soldier's dripping blood.
Be sensual, be mysterious, O my Night.

Be sensual, be mysterious, O my Night:
Impress me heavily with your flaccid might.
Let there be murmurs, jangled shrieks in the air,
Raving madness, and voluptuous despair!
Be sensual, be mysterious, O my Night.

Be sensual, be mysterious, O my Night:
Crawl over the sky in your vast shell void of light,
Old tortoise, for such maniacs as I,
Who never have learned to live and cannot die.
Be sensual, be mysterious, O my Night.
