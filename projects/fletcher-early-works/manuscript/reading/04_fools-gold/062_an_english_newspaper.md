## Original Lineation

(Adapted from Pushkin.)

Half-slaves and half-knaves and half-fools edit
        me:
And so, I am wholly a nonentity.

## Editorial Relineation

(Adapted from Pushkin.)

Half-slaves and half-knaves and half-fools edit me:
And so, I am wholly a nonentity.

## Context Notes

Two lines in a single rhyming couplet. The heading '(Adapted from Pushkin)' is in the source text; Alexander Pushkin (1799–1837) wrote political epigrams in this epigrammatic manner. l. 14 is a turnover line in the source; the editorial text restores the full line.
