## Original Lineation

(Lines written in an Inn on the Great Saint
Bernard Pass.)

Napoleon took his breakfast here,
And after rode afar:
Napoleon conquered Italy,
I wage internal war.

## Context Notes

Four lines in a single ballad quatrain; the source text carries the subtitle '(Lines written in an Inn on the Great Saint Bernard Pass).' Napoleon crossed the Pass in May 1800 with 40,000 troops, commemorated in David's *Napoleon Crossing the Alps* (1800–1801).
