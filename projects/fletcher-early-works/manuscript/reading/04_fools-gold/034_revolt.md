## Original Lineation

I cannot be at ease which way I take,
I have been chained in feebleness too long:
And so I cry a famished cry and strong,
Come clouds and from your sides let thunder break.

Shatter the cities and the fields uproot:
Either destroy me or destroy this hell,
It matters not, but break at least the spell
Of this fair-seeming summer without fruit.

I will not rest in safety, nor apart:
I must share life with all men, or life must
Rend song to atoms and my dream to dust.
God, let Your lightning pierce me to the heart.
