## Original Lineation

In the night dim
Shadows slim,--
Men--pass by:
Let them pass!
Lift the glass;
Drunken am I!

In the night dark,
With spark on spark
The cities blaze:
In dizzying flight
I dance to the light,
To wine my praise!

In night or day,
Sad or gay,
Low or high:
Weep if you will!
I am laughing still;--
Drunken was I!

## Context Notes

Three six-line stanzas in dimeter/trimeter, rhyming AABCCB. l. 6 ('Drunken am I!') is echoed and shifted to past tense at l. 30 ('Drunken was I!').
